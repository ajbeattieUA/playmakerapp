Team

Playmaker was made by 3 students for CS495 at the University of Alabama.

Austin Beattie

Austin is a recent graduate with a degree in Computer Science from the University of Alabama. He created most of the Playmaker interface, including the playbook, the play viewer, the team viewer, and profile viewer. Austin also implemented multi-threading in the playbook for better performance.
Brock Matter

Brock is a senior at the University of Alabama pursuing a degree in both Computer Science and Finance. Brock created the Play Editor, play animation, and much of the database that allows plays to be saved.
Daniel Redmond

Daniel is studying Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics. He has a preference for the C programming language. He focused on implementing the team and account management functionality of Playmaker.
